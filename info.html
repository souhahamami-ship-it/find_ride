About Us

RideShare is a digital ride-sharing platform developed to connect travelers across Tunisia. Our mission is to provide affordable, safe, and eco-friendly transportation options while reducing traffic congestion and carbon emissions.

Our Vision

We aim to become Tunisia's leading carpooling platform by promoting sustainable travel and community-based mobility solutions.

Our Values

  • Safety First
  • Community Trust
  • Environmental Responsibility
  • Affordable Mobility

Safety

Your safety is our top priority. RideShare implements several measures to ensure secure travel experiences:

  • Verified user registration
  • Driver and passenger rating system
  • Profile identity validation
  • Secure communication within the platform

We strongly encourage users to review profiles and ratings before confirming a ride.
